Welcome to ACM!

We are the official Computer Science Club at UMass Lowell. We host weekly event, open to all majors, such as:

  • Lectures from Industry Speakers
  • Technical Workshops
  • Student-run projects and hackathons
  • Trips and conferences
  • Internship connections, community involvement, and social events

Having a major or minor in Computer Science is in no way required. For those without a background in programming, there may be concepts and terminology that are unfamiliar. The goal of this club is to help students with learning subjects outside of their comfort zone. We have room for everyone.
